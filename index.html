Bootcamp 2020: US Senate Voting Helper

The US Senate Voting Helper is the project that concludes my 2 weeks of the Programming Bootcamp at City. It is a program that helps people with the voting process with the upcoming Elections in the USA. It will tell you who your Senators are when you enter your State and it will also tell you what year that they are up for Election.This Programming project has helped me to develop and understanding of conditionals,variables and user interaction.Whilst you are typing your State name there will be music playing in the background to help you through the process and there will be Images depicting what you are voting. I hope you have fun with your project and please read the README file before using the program.
